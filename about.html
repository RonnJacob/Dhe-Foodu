A website for all you foodies of Kozhikode.


From the world-famous Malabar Biryani to Kozhikodan Halwa, food surrounds one when in Kozhikode. Lying in the heartland of the spice coast, this city still continues to maintain it's tradition of delivering sumptuous delicacies to anybody who sets foot in it. Keeping with the trends of the digital era, DheFoodu is an effort to bring the tastes of Kozhikode online, making it available for everyone across the world.
